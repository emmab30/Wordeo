  • Tuls

    The tuls are virtual currencies. You generate coins: playing games, receiving approval of your loaded questions, sending suggestions, and more!

  • Challenges

    The challenges allow you to generate tuls and experience points. Each challenge has a goal, and by fulfilling that goal you unlock your medal!

  • Medals

    The medals are unlocked fulfilling challenges. They will allow you to have a status and some respect in front of your enemies!

  • VIP Rooms

    VIP rooms are rooms generated intelligently. You will receive a notification, enter and duplicate, and even four times the results of the game!

  • Experience

    Experience points help you level up. When you pass level you also win tuls and the odd surprise prize!

  • Ranking

    The ranking is your position within the entire Wordeo community. Another sign that will help you generate respect!

  • Characters

    You can buy new characters and customize them to your liking, adding accessories such as hats, feet, hands, horns.

  • Vitals

    The vitals are accessories that will help your monster to be in shape. You must nourish it and hydrate it so that your monster does not lose its life!
